Our heart for Port Colborne

A warm community
in Port Colborne

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ, equip believers for ministry, and reach our community and the world with the hope of the gospel.

What we believe

Our Faith & Values

  • We believe in the Holy Trinity, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God.
  • We believe in salvation through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • We believe in water baptism as a public declaration of faith.
  • We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues.
  • We believe in divine healing through the power of Jesus Christ.
  • We believe in the imminent, physical return of Jesus Christ.
  • We are affiliated with the Pentecostal Assemblies of Canada (PAOC).
  • We partner with MCS Bible College as a local hub for theological training.

Our Statement of Faith

Our Core Beliefs

As a Pentecostal church affiliated with the PAOC, our faith is rooted in Scripture and expressed in the following core beliefs.

  • We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.
  • We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and ascension.
  • We believe salvation comes through repentance and faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.
  • We believe in water baptism by immersion as a public declaration of faith.
  • We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the initial evidence of speaking in other tongues.
  • We believe in the ongoing gifts of the Holy Spirit for the Church today.
  • We believe in divine healing through the redemptive work of Christ.
  • We believe in the imminent, physical, and personal return of Jesus Christ.
  • We believe in the resurrection of the dead, eternal life for the saved, eternal separation for the lost.

Our Affiliation

of Canada

← Back to About Us

PAOC

Part of Something Larger

NorthPark Worship Centre is proudly affiliated with the Pentecostal Assemblies of Canada, one of the country's largest Evangelical denominations.

The PAOC is a fellowship of over 1,100 churches and 250,000 adherents across Canada, united by a common faith, a commitment to the authority of Scripture, and the empowering work of the Holy Spirit.

Our affiliation connects us to a national network of churches, mission workers, and ministry resources, so that the work we do locally is part of a much larger story of God's kingdom advancing across Canada and around the world.

Local Training, National College

NorthPark serves as a local hub for Master's College and Seminary, bringing accredited theological education right here to Port Colborne.

MCS offers degree and certificate programs in ministry, theology, and leadership, designed for those who want to deepen their knowledge of Scripture and grow in their calling, whether for vocational ministry or personal growth.

As a local hub, NorthPark makes it possible to access quality theological education without leaving your community. Contact us to find out what's currently available.
